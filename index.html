About Me

Nathan Rubin is a writer, athlete, economist, and storyteller with a passion for inspiring the next generation. When he’s not on the basketball court, he’s pouring his heart into writing—whether it’s poetry, reflections on life, or stories that uplift children. Nathan currently studies Economics, Community Relations, and Diaspora at the Liberal Arts College of the University of Massachusetts - Boston

His debut children’s book was created with one goal in mind: to spark imagination while teaching lessons of confidence, kindness, and self-belief. Nathan believes stories have the power to plant seeds of strength and joy in young readers, helping them see themselves as heroes in their own lives.

Through his book, Nathan hopes to not only entertain but also connect with kids and families, creating moments of laughter, learning, and inspiration

Chasing a Dream

Chasing a Dream

Nathan Rubin’s debut children’s book is a heartfelt story that blends fun, imagination, and important life lessons. Written to inspire confidence, kindness, and self-belief, the book encourages young readers to see the greatness within themselves.

Chasing a Dream is meant to be accessible to every child. Look for copies in local libraries, community centers, and youth organizations.

You can also check for free book-sharing locations using the official Little Free Library map.
